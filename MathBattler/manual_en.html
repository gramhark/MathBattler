How to Play

Your guide to MathBattler — the arithmetic battle RPG where math is your weapon.

🇯🇵 The game interface is in Japanese, but the gameplay is pure math — you're very welcome to play!
Overview Basic Flow Battle System Monster Types Equipment & Items Tips

01   Overview

Overview

🧮

Math-powered combat

Solve arithmetic problems (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division) to attack monsters. Get the answer right and your attack lands!

🗺️

100-floor dungeon

The dungeon has 100 floors. Each floor has 10 monsters, and the 10th is always a boss. Clear them all and push deeper!

⭐

Three difficulty levels

★, ★★, and ★★★. ★ focuses on basic arithmetic; ★★★ brings harder problems and tighter time limits. Go at your own pace.

📖

Collect monsters

Every defeated monster is recorded in your Monster Note. Rare species roam the dungeon — can you find them all?

02   Basic Flow

Basic Flow

1

Start from the Main Menu

Tap the "Battle" button to head to the dungeon selector. You can also check your equipment and items here.

2

Choose a floor and difficulty

Pick any floor from 1 to 100 and a difficulty (★ to ★★★). Floors you have cleared are recorded and saved.

3

Battle with math!

When a monster appears, a math problem is shown. Enter your answer on the number pad and tap the confirm button. Correct → you attack. Wrong → the monster hits you.

4

Defeat 10 monsters to clear the floor!

Beat all monsters on a floor to reach the Results screen. Your clear time is recorded, and the next floor unlocks.

03   Battle

Battle System

Step 1   Your Turn (Attack)

Answer the math problem

Tap number buttons to enter your answer.
Use the ✕ button to delete a digit.
Tap the ○ button to confirm and attack!

Step 2   Monster's Turn (Dodge)

Solve to dodge the attack

The monster is about to strike! Answer correctly to dodge entirely — no damage taken. Wrong answer or time-up means you take a hit.

Bonus   Critical Hit!

Answer fast for extra damage

On your attack turn, answer within the time limit for a Critical Hit — dealing 1.5× damage!

⚡

Critical time window

★: within 5 seconds  |  ★★: 4 seconds  |  ★★★: 3 seconds.
The harder the difficulty, the tighter the window!

🔥

Special Move

Dodge 4 times in a row on monster turns to charge your Special Move gauge! On your next attack turn, the number pad glows red — enter your answer to unleash a 2× damage special attack!

04   Monsters

Monster Types

Normal

Normal Monsters

The common inhabitants of the dungeon. They get tougher on deeper floors!

Heal

Heal Monsters

Defeat one to restore HP — a lucky encounter! More likely to appear when your HP is low.

Rare

Dungeon Rare

Unusual monsters that sometimes appear. Defeating one may drop rare equipment!

Super Rare

Super Rare

Only a 1% encounter rate. Consider yourself lucky if you spot one — make sure to add it to your Note!

Boss

Boss Monsters

The 10th monster on every floor is always a boss. High HP and hard-hitting — some even change form when weakened…

Special

Special

Mysterious monsters with unique effects. Their abilities are a secret — meet them and find out!

05   Items

Equipment & Items

Sword (Attack boost)

Dungeon drop / Shop purchase

Equipping a sword increases damage dealt to monsters. Find better swords as dungeon drops or buy them from the Shop. Higher floors yield stronger gear.

Shield (Defense boost)

Dungeon drop / Shop purchase

A shield reduces damage received from monsters. Even when you fail to dodge, a good shield keeps things manageable. Grab one alongside your sword!

Items (usable during battle)

Shop purchase / Dungeon drop

Consumables you can use mid-battle. Keep a few in your bag for when things get tough!

Heal Orb (restores HP)
Attack Orb (up to 3× per monster, boosts attack)
Defense Orb (up to 3× per monster, boosts defense)
Spike Orb (deals damage to the enemy)
Poison Orb (inflicts poison)
Paralyze Orb (inflicts paralysis)
Stone Orb (inflicts petrification)

06   Tips

Tips

💡 Start on ★

Difficulty ★ focuses on addition and subtraction. Get comfortable with the flow first, then take on ★★ when you're ready!

🛡️ Use items when HP is low

Heal Monsters appear more often when your HP is low, which helps — but don't rely on luck alone. Keep a Heal Orb in your bag just in case.

📖 Fill your Monster Note

Every monster you defeat is recorded. Try different floors to encounter all types and aim for a complete collection!

⚠️ Back up your save data!

Save data is stored in your browser. Clearing browser cache or switching to a different browser can wipe it. Use the in-game backup feature to keep your progress safe.
